How to play (streamlined Russian Bank / Crapette)

  • Each player has their own deck. Goal: be the first to empty your Stock pile.
  • On your turn, you may move the top card of your Stock, your Waste, or any of your Tableau columns onto:
    • A Foundation (shared, build up A→K by suit), or
    • Any Tableau column — yours or your opponent's — if it's one rank lower and the opposite color of that column's top card (or the column is empty).
  • Dumping cards on your opponent's tableau is the core attack of Crapette — it blocks their columns and slows them down!
  • Make as many legal moves as you like. When you have no more moves (or choose to stop), click Draw to flip a card from your Hand to your Waste — this ends your turn.
  • If your Hand runs out, clicking Draw reshuffles your Waste back into your Hand (face down) without ending your turn.
  • Click a card to select it (highlighted gold), then click a destination pile to play it. Click it again to deselect.
  • First player to empty their Stock pile wins!
